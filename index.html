List of keyword shortcuts:

Keys [0-9] and '.' are for values.

Operations:

  • +: is for plus.
  • -: is for minus.
  • /: is for division.
  • *: is for multiplication.
  • r: is for square root.
  • p: is for power.
  • m: is for module.

Backspace will delete a part of the display.

Delete will reset the calculator.

Enter will compute the result.
